Uniform Acceleration

Radiation of a uniformly accelerated charge is a persistent problem in the physics literature. There are several paradoxes which arise including the following.

  • Nonzero power radiated predicted by the Larmor formula, yet 0 radiation reaction
  • Equivalence principle paradox
For an overview of this area, I recommend reading: Fulton & Rohrlich, Boulware, Pauri & Vallisneri.
To become aquainted with the equivalence principle paradox, the papers by Pauri & Vallisneri, Ginzburg (1970), and Fulling & Wilson are great sources.
For work on the scalar field, Ren & Weinberg is the best source, others including Fulling & Wilson (in 2d spacetime) also have contributions in the scalar case.
