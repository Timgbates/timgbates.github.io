Fibonacci Functor

Let us consider the category where the objects are natural numbers and the morphisms is the usual partial ordering on $\mathbb{N}$ (so there exists an arrow $a \rightarrow b$ if $a \leq b$). Consider the map $F: \mathbb{N} \rightarrow \mathbb{N}$ defined by $n \mapsto f_n$, the nth Fibonacci number.
